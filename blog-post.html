10 Hidden Gems in Santorini Only Locals Know About

📅 April 5, 2024 ⏱️ 5 min read 👤 By Admin

Santorini is undoubtedly one of the most beautiful islands in the world, famous for its stunning sunsets, white-washed buildings, and blue-domed churches. However, beyond the crowded streets of Oia and Fira lies a treasure trove of hidden gems that only locals know about.

1. Pyrgos Village

While most tourists flock to Oia for sunset views, Pyrgos offers an equally stunning but far less crowded alternative. This medieval village sits at the highest point of Santorini and provides panoramic views of the entire island.

2. Vlychada Beach

Often called the "Moon Beach" due to its unique lunar landscape, Vlychada remains one of Santorini's best-kept secrets. The white volcanic cliffs create a dramatic backdrop.

3. Megalochori

This traditional village offers an authentic glimpse into Santorinian life. With its neoclassical mansions and traditional wine canavas, it's perfect for a leisurely afternoon.
